🪟 Screen Cleaning

We always recommend washing screens during your window cleaning appointment. Two convenient methods are available:

Method A — Full Removal & Wash

We remove screens from their tracks and wash them thoroughly inside your apartment (protecting your floors with towels) or in the backyard using a hose and brushes. After drying, we re-install them in their tracks.

$3 – $5 per screen

Method B — In-Place Cleaning

We wet the screen while it remains on the window, then wipe with a special dry cloth that absorbs dirt. This method is efficient, cost-effective, and requires no removal. Ideal for hard-to-reach or sealed screen installations.

$2 – $3 per screen
