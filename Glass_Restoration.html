Glass Restoration Services

When Cleaning Isn't Enough

Over time, glass accumulates damage that standard cleaning can't fix — hard water mineral deposits, calcium buildup, oxidation from metal frames, and light surface scratches. Our glass restoration service uses professional-grade polishing compounds and techniques to remove these defects.

In most cases, we can restore glass to near-original clarity without replacement. This saves our clients significant money while delivering dramatic visual improvements — particularly valuable for commercial storefronts, shower doors, high-rise facades, and historic properties.
