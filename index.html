Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our window cleaning services

Window cleaning costs vary based on the number of windows, building height, accessibility, and condition. Residential services typically start at $6–$12 per window for standard cleaning. Commercial pricing is customized based on building size and frequency. We offer free, no-obligation quotes — call us at (800) 496-0744 or fill out our contact form.
For residential properties, we recommend professional window cleaning at least twice a year — typically in spring and fall. Commercial properties and storefronts benefit from monthly or quarterly cleaning to maintain a professional appearance. High-traffic or coastal locations may need more frequent service due to increased exposure to grime and salt.
Yes, Prime Window Cleaning is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We carry comprehensive general liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage. All of our technicians are trained and certified, and we comply with all OSHA and NYC Department of Buildings safety requirements for both low-rise and high-rise work.
Yes! We offer interior and exterior window cleaning for both residential and commercial properties. Interior cleaning includes the glass, sills, frames, and tracks. Exterior cleaning covers the glass surface and frames. We also clean screens, storm windows, and skylights upon request.
Rain on clean windows is actually not a problem! Rain itself is relatively clean water. What causes spots on dirty windows is rain mixing with existing dirt and grime on the glass. Professionally cleaned windows will remain clean even after rainfall. However, if you're not satisfied for any reason, our satisfaction guarantee means we'll return to make it right.
We serve all five NYC boroughs (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, The Bronx, and Staten Island), Long Island (Nassau and Suffolk Counties), and Northern/Central New Jersey. Our primary service area extends up to 2 hours from our Brooklyn headquarters, but we travel further for larger projects. View all service areas →
Absolutely. We use biodegradable, eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are tough on grime but gentle on the environment. Our products are safe for pets, children, and plants. We also use purified water-fed pole systems for exterior cleaning, which eliminate the need for chemical detergents entirely.
