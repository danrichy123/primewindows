Why Choose Us

Clean windows.
Flawless results.
Every time.

Since 2002, Prime Window Cleaning NYC has been raising the bar for residential and commercial window washing across Brooklyn and New York City. From day one, we knew that simply hanging a shingle wouldn't be enough — so we set out to build something genuinely different.

That commitment drove us to invest in the finest equipment, hire only the most skilled technicians, and develop a service culture obsessed with exceeding expectations. Whether we're polishing the windows of a cozy brownstone or tackling the towering glass facade of a Manhattan high-rise, our standard never wavers: the cleanest windows in the city, period.

Over more than two decades, we've continuously expanded our capabilities — giving homeowners and business owners alike the freedom to focus on what matters most to them.
